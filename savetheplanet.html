Slightly fewer Americans concerned about multiple environmental problems: Gallup

1m ago

TNK

Fewer Americans in a new poll are concerned about multiple environmental problems, with small declines seen in reported worries since last year. The Gallup poll found that 55 percent of Americans say they have a “great deal” of worry about the pollution of drinking water in the U.S., down from 57 percent last year.

‘Frightening’: record-busting heat and drought hit Europe in 2022

1m ago

TNK

The climate crisis had “frightening” impacts in Europe last year, with heatwaves killing more than 20,000 people and drought withering crops, an EU report has found. Its writers said drought was already baked in for many farmers in 2023. The only way to limit the rising damages of global heating was rapidly to cut carbon emissions, they said.
